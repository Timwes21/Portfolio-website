The Batman

Source Code



The player must beat all enemies in each wave. As you progress, your health increases. but so does the diffulcty of the waves. With a small arsenal of smoke grenades and batarangs, along with your martial arts prowess, the enemies have as much fighting chance as you!


Characters


Batman

Controls:

Move: Left/Right arrow

Jump: Up arrow

Punch: A

Kick: S

Super Punch: D

Batarang: Q

Smoke Grenade: W

Block: Space Bar

Knife Guy

Waves: 1 - 5

Attack: Stabs player when in striking distance


Gun Guy

Waves: 5 - 19

Attack: Shoots an array of bullets that can't be blocked


Mime

Waves: 8+

Attack: Round house kick when in striking distance


Axe Guy

Waves: 18+

Attack 1: Throws axe in the players direction that can't be blocked

Attack 2: Hits the player with an axe when in striking distance


Fire Mime

Waves: 23+
